Minecraft Skin Pose Editor

Pose Minecraft skins in 3D, then export or share the result.

MC Poser is a browser-based tool for loading Minecraft skins from a username or PNG, adjusting character poses with per-joint controls, exporting polished renders, and sharing projects with compact links.

Key features

  • Username-based Minecraft skin lookup
  • PNG upload with slim, classic, or auto-detect arms
  • Interactive 3D posing with camera and lighting controls
  • Image export settings for size, format, and background
  • Share links for projects and rendered images

How it works

  1. Load a Minecraft skin from a username or local PNG file.
  2. Pose the model in the 3D editor and fine-tune the framing.
  3. Export an image or share the scene with a generated link.
